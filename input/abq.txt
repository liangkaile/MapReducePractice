Albuquerque is the largest city in the state of New Mexico, United
States. It is the county seat of Bernalillo County and is situated in the central part of the state,
straddling the Rio Grande. The city population was 545,852 as of the 2010 Census and ranks as
the 32nd-largest city in the U.S. It has a metropolitan population of 887,077 as of the 2010 Census.
Albuquerque is the 57th-largest United States metropolitan area.
